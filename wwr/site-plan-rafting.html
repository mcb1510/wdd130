Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to capture the customers' attention persuading them to use these services by providing clear information, prices, and actual visual content. This will benefit the rafting company because customers will have a clear and honest idea of what they are getting when utilizing the service.

Audience

The type of Audience that would visit this website will be families looking for different things to do during summer, college students looking for hobbies, and current rafting customers who are looking for a high-quality service. The content, colors, fonts, etc. will create a striking, shining, and friendly impression.
